## 📌 Project Title  
**Legal, Ethical & User Agreement Analysis of Rokomari.com**

---

## 🎯 Purpose of the Project  
This project aims to critically analyze the **Terms of Service (ToS), Privacy Policy, and User Agreements** of Rokomari.com in the context of **Bangladeshi cybersecurity laws and ethical standards**.  

The goal is to:
- Evaluate **legal compliance** with national regulations  
- Identify **privacy and data protection gaps**  
- Assess **user rights and platform responsibilities**  
- Compare practices with **industry standards and best practices**  

---

## 📊 Poster Overview  
The poster presents a structured analysis covering:

- 🔍 Transparency & Accessibility of Agreements  
- 🔐 Privacy & Data Protection  
- ⚖️ Compliance with Laws & Regulations  
- 📚 Intellectual Property & Content  
- ⚠️ Role & Liability Disclaimer  
- 👤 User Rights & Responsibilities  
- 🚫 Prohibited Activities & Enforcement  
- 🔄 Refunds & Dispute Resolution  
- 📈 Compliance Scorecard  
- 💡 Key Recommendations

## ⚖️ Legal Framework Covered  

- ICT Act 2006  
- Digital Security Act 2018  
- Cyber Security Act 2023  
- Consumer Rights Protection Act 2009  
- National Digital Commerce Policy 2018  
- Digital Commerce Guidelines 2021  

---

## 🧠 Key Findings  

- Partial compliance with Bangladeshi legal frameworks  
- Weak implementation of **data privacy and consent mechanisms**  
- Lack of **clear dispute resolution and refund policies (digital content)**  
- Limited **transparency and accessibility of policies**  
- No alignment with **global standards (GDPR/CCPA)**  

---

## 💡 Recommendations  

- Introduce **explicit user consent mechanisms**  
- Provide **Bengali versions of policies**  
- Define **data retention policies clearly**  
- Implement **ADR (Alternative Dispute Resolution)**  
- Improve **transparency and notification systems**
